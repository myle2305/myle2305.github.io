Palat Tuntaopas is a freelance web designer based in Thailand with a passion for creating artistic websites. Palat believes that web design can be an art form and is committed to pushing the boundaries of what is thought possible in this field.

1. What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

The most noticeable element was the author's choice of a high-contrast color palette. It gives the site a really lively feel. But it's more than just a vibrancy. The way the colors are arranged, the overall, and how the highlighted text is layered - it's all distinctive.

Users who want to see how the elements interact have to move the cursor around. It sparks a sense of curiosity making users want to explore the site further and discover what else is going on.
